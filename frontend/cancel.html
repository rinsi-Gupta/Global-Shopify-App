Cancellation & Return Policy

Last Updated: August 1, 2025

Cancellation Policy

  • Orders can be canceled within 24 hours of placement.
  • Orders already processed may not be eligible for cancellation.
  • Contact us at support@GlobalShopify.com for cancellation.
  • Refunds processed within 7 business days of approval.
  • Custom or personalized items cannot be canceled.

Return Policy

  • Returns accepted within 7 days of delivery.
  • Items must be unused and in original packaging.
  • No returns on customized/personalized products.
  • Report defects/damages with pictures immediately.
  • Refunds or replacements processed within 10 business days.
  • Sale items and gift cards are non-returnable.

Exchange Policy

  • Only applicable for defective/damaged items.
  • Email us at support@GlobalShopify.com within 7 days.
  • Customer bears return shipping unless due to our error.

How to Initiate a Return?

  1. Contact us at support@GlobalShopify.com with order details.
  2. We’ll send return instructions and address.
  3. Use a trackable shipping method to send the item.
  4. After inspection, we’ll process your refund or exchange.
  5. Refund is issued post product verification.

Refund Policy

  • Refunds issued to original payment method within 7–10 business days.
  • Shipping charges are non-refundable (unless due to error).
  • Contact us if refund is delayed beyond timeline.
  • COD refunds via bank transfer or store credit.
  • Prepaid refunds go back to original payment source.

Late or Missing Refunds

  • Check your bank account or card statement first.
  • Contact your bank if refund isn't reflected.
  • Still no refund? Email us at support@GlobalShopify.com.

Need Help?

For any cancellation, return, or refund inquiries, contact us at support@GlobalShopify.com or call +1234567890.
