Shipping Policies

Last Updated: August 1, 2025

Shipping Timeframe

Orders are processed within 1-3 business days. Delivery time varies based on location, typically between 5-10 business days.

Shipping Charges

We offer free shipping on orders above $50. Standard shipping rates apply for orders below this amount.

International Shipping

We ship worldwide! International shipping rates vary by country and will be calculated at checkout.

Order Tracking

Once your order is shipped, you will receive a tracking number via email to monitor its status.

Delayed Shipments

Delays may occur due to unforeseen circumstances. If your order is delayed, contact us at support@GlobalShopify.com.

Lost or Damaged Items

If your package is lost or damaged in transit, please reach out to us immediately with your order details and pictures (if applicable).

Need Help?
