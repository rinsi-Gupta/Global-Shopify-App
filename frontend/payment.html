Payment Policies

Last Updated: August 1, 2025

Shipping Methods

  • Standard Shipping (5-7 Business Days): Cost-effective delivery for domestic orders.
  • Express Shipping (2-3 Business Days): Faster delivery for urgent orders, available at an additional cost.
  • Overnight Shipping (1 Business Day): Next-day delivery for extremely urgent orders.
  • International Shipping (7-14 Business Days): Available for customers worldwide.

Shipping Charges

Shipping costs depend on destination, package weight, and selected shipping method. Orders above $100 qualify for free standard shipping.

Order Processing

Orders are processed within 1–2 business days, excluding weekends and holidays. You will receive an email confirmation once your order is processed.

Tracking Your Order

Once your order is shipped, a tracking number will be emailed to you. Use this to monitor delivery status.

International Shipping

Customs fees, taxes, and import duties vary by country. These charges must be paid by the recipient.

Delayed Shipments

Shipments may be delayed due to weather, customs, or high-demand periods. Contact our support team for assistance.

Damaged or Lost Packages

If your package arrives damaged, take photos and contact our support team immediately.

Restricted Shipping Locations

We do not ship to PO boxes, APO/FPO addresses, or restricted regions. Contact us for more details.

Eco-Friendly Packaging

We use recyclable and biodegradable materials. Help us protect the environment.

Returns Due to Shipping Issues

If an order is returned due to an incorrect address or failed delivery attempts, the customer may be responsible for re-shipping fees.
