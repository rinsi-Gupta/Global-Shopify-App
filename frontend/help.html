Account Related Queries

For account related issues refer to the below questions to get the complete information and if you're still unable to access your account? Click on the Contact Us option to talk to our customer care.

How do I login to my GlobalShopify account? expand_more

Visit the GlobalShopify login page, enter your email and password, and click 'Login'. If you face issues, ensure your credentials are correct or use the 'Forgot Password' option.

Additional tip: Use two-factor authentication for added security.

I am not able to login to my GlobalShopify account. expand_more

Check your internet connection, verify your email and password, or reset your password using the 'Forgot Password' link.

Note: If issues persist, clear your browser cache or try a different device.

Why am I not getting an OTP? expand_more

Ensure your phone number is correct, check your signal, or request a new OTP. Contact support if the issue persists.

Tip: Verify your number is registered with your account.

I am not getting the Forgot Password link. expand_more

Check your spam/junk folder, verify your email, or try requesting the link again. Contact support for further assistance.

Additional help: Ensure your email provider isn’t blocking GlobalShopify emails.

Returns & Exchanges Queries

Learn about returning or exchanging items purchased from GlobalShopify. Contact support for assistance if needed.

What is the return policy for GlobalShopify? expand_more

GlobalShopify offers a 30-day return policy for unused items in original packaging. Initiate a return via your account under 'Orders'.

Note: Items must be unopened and in resalable condition.

How do I start a return or exchange? expand_more

Log into your GlobalShopify account, go to 'Orders', select the item, and choose 'Return' or 'Exchange'. Follow the instructions provided.

Tip: Keep your order confirmation handy for a smoother process.

How can I track my return? expand_more

Track your return status in your GlobalShopify account under 'Returns' after initiating the process.

Additional info: You’ll receive a confirmation email with tracking details.

GlobalShopify Credit & Insider Queries

Explore benefits and queries related to GlobalShopify Credit and Insider program.

How do I check my GlobalShopify Credit balance? expand_more

Log into your GlobalShopify account and check your Credit balance under 'My Wallet'.

Note: Credits expire after 6 months if unused.

What are the benefits of GlobalShopify Insider? expand_more

Insider members get exclusive discounts, early access to sales, and free shipping. Join via your account settings.

Tip: Upgrade to Insider for premium benefits.

Offers Queries

Find answers to questions about offers and promotions on GlobalShopify.

How do I apply an offer on GlobalShopify? expand_more

Apply offers at checkout by entering the promo code in the 'Offers' section on the GlobalShopify cart page.

Note: Offers are valid only during the specified period.

What happens if an offer expires? expand_more

Expired offers cannot be applied. Check for new offers on the GlobalShopify website under 'Promotions'.

Tip: Sign up for notifications to stay updated on new offers.

Payments Queries

Get help with payment-related issues on GlobalShopify.

What payment methods are accepted by GlobalShopify? expand_more

GlobalShopify accepts credit/debit cards, UPI, net banking, and wallets. See all options at checkout.

Additional info: International cards may require verification.

Why did my payment fail? expand_more

Payment failures may occur due to insufficient funds or network issues. Retry or contact your bank, then try again on GlobalShopify.

Tip: Ensure your payment method is active and funded.

Cancellations & Charges Queries

Learn about cancelling orders and associated charges on GlobalShopify.

How do I cancel an order on GlobalShopify? expand_more

Cancel an order via your GlobalShopify account under 'Orders' before it ships. Post-shipment cancellations require a return.

Note: Cancellation is free if done before dispatch.

Are there charges for cancellations? expand_more

No charges apply for cancellations before shipping. Post-shipment cancellations may incur return shipping fees.

Tip: Check cancellation eligibility in your order details.

Frequently Asked Questions expand_more

How do I reset my password?

To reset your password, go to the login page and click "Forgot Password." Follow the instructions sent to your registered email.

Tip: Use a strong password with letters, numbers, and symbols.

How can I track my order?

You can track your order by logging into your account and visiting the "Orders" section. A tracking link will be provided once your order ships.

Note: Tracking updates may take up to 24 hours after shipping.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy for unused items in their original packaging. Please contact support to initiate a return.

Additional info: Returns are free within the first 15 days.
